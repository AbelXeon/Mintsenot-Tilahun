About Me

Hello! My name is Mintsenot Tilahun, and I am a highly motivated 3rd-year Computer Science student currently completing my degree at Infolink University College at the Hawassa Campus in Ethiopia. Ever since my introduction to computing, I have been fascinated by how structured logic and database algorithms can solve real-world system inefficiencies. For me, computer science is more than just an academic major—it is a creative medium where rigorous architecture and visual accessibility merge to solve complex problems.

Living and pursuing my education in Hawassa, a region actively experiencing digital growth across commercial and administrative sectors, gives me direct motivation. As I enter my final year of study, I am excited to collaborate on new digital tools, participate in software internships, and contribute to the rapidly expanding tech space in Ethiopia.
